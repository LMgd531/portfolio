1. Photoshoot Oct 2017: When I first got my DSLR, I wasn't thinking about how well it performed and wasn't too critical about the image quality. I watched a lot of videos on how to change settings, so I used manual settings from the start along with autofocus. Before getting my camera, I have watched a few videos on editing with Lightroom. I decided to get Lightroom and combine both tools to create better images. I used the radial tool a lot and changed the temperature to bring separation and contrast to the pictures, which I liked back then. _MG_2386 is one of my best images back then. I used the rule of thirds as a technique to improve my photos. The image '_MG_3185' was taken in November. I started using other features from Lightroom and really started to learn the software as a whole. I was excited about this result, and even now, I think this is a pretty well shot and edited photo.
