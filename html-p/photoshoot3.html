3. Photoshoot 27th Dec. 2017: This is the first and only time I went to London. My editing has improved, and I started creating more moody and cinematic looks on my photos. I feel like my compositions weren't the best and, I wasn't familiar with 'street photography' so, I took fewer shots than I would like to now. One other thing that I did wrong was setting the shutter speed to 1/5th of a second at night time. I never wanted to go over 500 ISO cause of quality. However, now I would rather use a maximum of 1600 ISO and a higher shutter speed than what I used back then.
