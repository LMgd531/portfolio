3. Project Blender House 2: This time, I wanted to create a house as close as I could to a reference. This took a lot more time than my previous projects. I wanted to get it as close as possible, and therefore I needed to get the same angle and focal length of the camera, find similar textures create the same height and look. These blender projects are so much fun. Having a goal and seeing the improvements was really enjoyable.
